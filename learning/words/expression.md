- 词性：noun

- 中文释义：表达；体现；表现

## 荒诞寓言

A quiet castle kept its treasures hidden underground. One day it built a tall glass tower so everyone could see what mattered inside. That tower was the expression of the castle's true value.

## 寓意

An expression makes an inner quality visible to other people.

## 记忆钩子

ex- means out; expression = press something out so it can be seen.

## 英文锚定句

A brand is the expression of essential characteristics and values of a product or service.

## 常见搭配

- the expression of an idea
- facial expression
- an expression of love

## 个人化例句

A simple logo can be an expression of a company's values.

## 易混词/注意点

In this sentence, expression means manifestation or embodiment, not a mathematical expression.
