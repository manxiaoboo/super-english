# role model

- 发音：/roʊl mɒdəl/
- 词性：noun (countable)
- 核心意思：a person whose behavior and success others admire and want to copy
- 核心动作/画面：一个人站在前面发光，旁边有人悄悄模仿他的每个动作
- 中文释义：榜样；楷模；值得效仿的人

## 荒诞寓言

有只小鸭子觉得自己走路太笨，于是它宣布：我要以火烈鸟为 role model。它开始每天单腿站立，把羽毛染成粉红色，学着昂首阔步。三个月后，它没有变成火烈鸟，但却成了鸭群里姿态最优雅、最自信的一只。大家都来问它：你是怎么做到的？它说：我选了一个比我优秀很多的 role model，然后一直努力追。

## 寓意

role model 不是让你变成别人，而是借助仰望的力量，让自己变得更好。

## 记忆钩子

role（角色）+ model（模型/样板）→ 生活剧本里你想扮演的那个角色的样板 → role model = 榜样

## 英文锚定句

He became my role model because he always delivered clean code and handled feedback with grace.

## 常见搭配

- be a role model for someone
- serve as / act as a role model
- look up to someone as a role model
- have / find a role model
- positive / negative role model

## 个人化例句

My tech lead serves as a role model for me — not just in coding skills, but in how he communicates clearly under pressure.

## 易混词/注意点

role model vs mentor: mentor actively guides you; role model is someone you admire and imitate, not necessarily someone who knows you. role model vs idol: idol implies worship; role model emphasizes behavior worth copying. Written as two separate words, no hyphen.
