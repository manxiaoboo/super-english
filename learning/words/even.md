# even

## 荒诞寓言

有台机器人厨师挑战所有料理，八层蛋糕都做成功了，even 连最难的分子料理也掌握了。观众刚要鼓掌，它又把一粒米雕成城堡。大家终于明白：even 是把惊讶再往上推一格。

## 寓意

even 用来强调超出预期的程度，让事情显得更强、更极端或更意外。

## 记忆钩子

even 本意像一条平线；作强调副词时，可以想象它把原来的水平线再往上推一格。这是记忆钩子，不是词源解释。

## 英文锚定句

This approach is even more efficient than I expected.

## 常见搭配

- even better / even worse / even faster
- even more useful
- even though it was difficult
- even if it takes longer
- not even close

## 个人化例句

This review method is even more useful when I turn each word into a reusable sentence pattern.

## 易混词/注意点

even as an emphasizing adverb usually goes before the word or phrase it modifies: She didn't even reply. Use even + comparative for stronger comparison: even better, even more efficient. even though means although; even if means whether or not.
