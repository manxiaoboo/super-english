# but

- 发音：/bʌt/
- 词性：conjunction / preposition / adverb
- 核心意思：introduces contrast or exception
- 核心动作/画面：前半句前进时遇到一堵转折小墙
- 中文释义：但是；然而；除了；只有

- 熟练度：new

- 复习次数：0

## 荒诞寓言

有台机器人厨师以为自己能复制所有美食，蛋糕、分子料理都成功了。but，它遇到一碗奶奶的手擀面后突然关机。那碗面像一堵小墙，挡住了它的自信，也推出了另一面：技术强，但温度难复制。

## 寓意

but 用来转向相反的一面，或指出例外和限制。

## 记忆钩子

but 像一堵小墙：前半句往前冲，撞到 but 后，意思转向另一边。这是视觉记忆钩子。

## 英文锚定句

I wanted to skip the meeting, but it turned out to be the most useful one all week.

## 常见搭配

- have no choice but to do something
- can't help but notice
- nothing but excuses
- everyone but the manager
- but for your help

## 个人化例句

I wanted to finish quickly, but I had to slow down and make the sentence sound natural.

## 易混词/注意点

but most often introduces contrast. In fixed chunks, use have no choice but to + verb and can't help but + bare verb. Do not say can't help but to do something. nothing but means only; everyone but X means everyone except X.
