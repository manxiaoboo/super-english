# mentally

- 发音：/mɛntəli/
- 词性：adverb
- 核心意思：in the mind; relating to thought, emotion, or psychological state
- 核心动作/画面：闭上眼睛，在脑子里独自承受或准备某件事
- 中文释义：在精神上；在心理上；用脑子；从心理角度

## 荒诞寓言

一位举重冠军每天练四小时，能举起两百公斤。比赛前，教练问：你准备好了吗？他说：身体完全准备好了。教练又说：那你脑子里呢？他愣了一下，从没想过这个问题。上台后，对手只是盯了他一眼，他的手就开始发抖，两百公斤举了一半就掉了。裁判摇头说：他在身体上训练了一年，但 mentally 他从未准备过。

## 寓意

mentally 指的是心理和精神层面的状态，跟身体的准备是两回事。

## 记忆钩子

mental（心理的）+ -ly（副词后缀）→ mentally = 在心理上、用脑子。mental 联想 mental hospital（精神病院）→ 和大脑、心理有关的一切。

## 英文锚定句

I was not mentally prepared for how difficult the code review would be.

## 常见搭配

- mentally prepared / mentally ready
- mentally strong / mentally tough
- mentally exhausted / mentally drained
- mentally challenging / mentally stimulating
- mentally ill / mentally healthy
- mentally focused

## 个人化例句

Reading English documentation for hours is not just tiring physically — I feel mentally drained by the end of the day.

## 易混词/注意点

mentally vs emotionally: mentally = cognitive/thinking layer; emotionally = feelings layer. mentally vs intellectually: intellectually is more about academic ability; mentally covers broader psychological state. Note: mentally ill is fixed collocation — do not say mentally sick.
