# reward

- 发音：/rɪwɔːrd/
- 词性：noun / verb
- 核心意思：something given in return for good work or effort; to give something in recognition of achievement
- 核心动作/画面：付出之后，有东西流回来——努力→奖励，一个完整的循环
- 中文释义：奖励；回报；酬劳；（动词）奖励某人、给予回报

## 荒诞寓言

有只实验室小狗学会了握手，得到一块饼干作为 reward。它觉得不够，于是学翻滚，得到两块。它越来越贪心，开始研究微积分，背单词，优化算法。三年后，它已经是公司最资深的工程师。CEO 来问它：你为什么这么努力？它抬起爪子说：因为每一次进步，我都得到了 reward。人类说：那你现在想要什么奖励？它想了一秒，回答：再来一块饼干就够了。

## 寓意

reward 是努力完成循环的那个东西——它可以很小，但它必须真实存在，才能驱动持续行动。

## 记忆钩子

re-（回来）+ ward（方向）→ 你付出的努力，有东西回到你这里 → reward = 回报、奖励（注：这是记忆钩子，非严格词源。）

## 英文锚定句

The company rewards employees who take initiative with more responsibility and growth opportunities.

## 常见搭配

- receive / get a reward
- offer / give a reward
- as a reward for something
- reward someone for doing something
- reward someone with something
- financial / intrinsic / extrinsic reward
- rewarding experience / rewarding work

## 个人化例句

I find it deeply rewarding when a feature I built gets positive feedback from real users.

## 易混词/注意点

reward vs prize: prize is for competition; reward is broader return for effort or contribution, not necessarily competitive. reward vs bonus: bonus is monetary workplace reward; reward can be non-monetary. Adjective form: rewarding = meaningful, worthwhile, giving a sense of achievement.
