## Meaning

to be able or allowed to use, reach, or open a system, file, server, or resource

## Usage Context

- 中文：有权限访问；可以使用；能够进入
- 常见对象：server, system, file, folder, platform, database
- 技术语境里很高频
- 比 can visit 更自然，因为重点是权限和可访问性，不是去‘拜访’某个地方

## Anchor Sentence

Only some users have access to that server.
