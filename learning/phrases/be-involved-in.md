## Meaning

to take part in something or be connected with it

## Usage Context

- 中文：参与……；卷入……；和……有关
- 既可以表示主动参与，也可以表示被卷入某件事，具体看上下文
- 常见对象：project, discussion, decision-making, accident, scam, process
- 比 join 更强调已经处在事情里面、和事情存在实际关联
- 常见用法：be involved in a project / be heavily involved in / get involved in the discussion

## Anchor Sentence

I want to be involved in projects that improve real learning.

## Review Questions

1. be involved in 的核心意思是什么？
2. 它和 join 或 take part in 相比，强调的区别是什么？
3. 用 be involved in 写一句和你的英语学习、工作或项目有关的句子。
