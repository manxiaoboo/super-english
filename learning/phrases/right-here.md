## Meaning

exactly here; in this exact place

## Usage Context

- 中文：就在这里
- 核心强调：强调位置非常明确，不是别处，就是这里
- 常见语气：精确定位、提醒别人别找错地方、强调自己就在现场
- 常见用法：Put it right here. / The answer is right here. / I'm right here with you.

## Anchor Sentence

The answer is right here in the document.
