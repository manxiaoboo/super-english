# A is the expression of B

## Meaning

A 是 B 的体现，而不是 B 本身。

## Usage Context

适合定义概念、说明某种外在形式如何表现内在特征、价值、情感或思想。

## Anchor Sentence

A brand is the expression of essential characteristics and values of a product or service.

## Review Questions
