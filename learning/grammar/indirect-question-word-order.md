## Meaning

When a wh-question becomes a noun clause (indirect question), the word order changes from V+S to S+V — no inversion inside the clause.

## Usage Context

核心规则：

直接问句（倒装）：
What is his job?
Where is the server?
Why did the build fail?

间接从句（名词从句 + 陈述语序）：
... what his job is
... where the server is
... why the build failed

关键：疑问词 what/where/why/who/how 后面的部分永远是 主语 + 谓语 的陈述顺序，不要倒装。

常见触发动词：know, find out, tell me, understand, see, ask, wonder, check, explain, decide

## Anchor Sentence

I need to find out what his job is. (不是 what is his job)

## Review Questions

1. 直接问句 What is his job? 变成名词从句后语序是什么？为什么？
2. 把下面这个直接问句改写成 find out 后面的名词从句：Where is the server?
3. 用 I need to find out why... 造一句和你工作有关的句子。
