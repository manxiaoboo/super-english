# experience with or in not for

## Meaning

experience + with/in + 名词/动名词 表示对某事物有经验；experience + for 不搭配

## Usage Context

用于表达在某领域/工具/活动上的经验。正确：experience with coding / experience in this field。错误：experience for this job。

## Anchor Sentence

I have a lot of experience with Python. / She has years of experience in teaching.

## Review Questions

1. 填空：I don't have much experience ___ this kind of work. (填入正确介词)
2. 改错：He has no experience for managing teams.
3. 用 experience + with 造句：你在某方面有经验
