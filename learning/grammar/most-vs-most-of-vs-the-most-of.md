# most vs most of vs the most of

## Meaning

Most + 复数名词 = 大多数(泛指); Most of the + 复数名词 = 某一特定群体中的大多数(特指); the most of 这个结构不存在。

## Usage Context

Most children like games.(泛指所有孩子)
Most of the children in this class like games.(特指这个班的孩子)
The most of children 是错误用法。

## Anchor Sentence

Most people enjoy music. / Most of the people I know enjoy music.

## Review Questions

1. 填空：______ children need at least 9 hours of sleep. (Most / Most of / The most of)
2. 填空：______ the students in my class passed the exam. (Most / Most of / The most of)
3. 改错：The most of my friends live in this city.
