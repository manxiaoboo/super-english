# the developer who fixed the bug

## Chinese Intention

修复了这个 bug 的开发者

## Natural Chunk

the developer who fixed the bug

## Avoid Saying

- the developer who fixes bugs
- that developer who fixed bugs

## Sentence Patterns

- I know the developer who ...
- The developer who ... is ...

## Real-Life Examples

- I know the developer who fixed the bug.

## Usage Notes

Use the developer who fixed the bug when talking about one specific past bug. Use fixed, not fixes, for a completed action.
