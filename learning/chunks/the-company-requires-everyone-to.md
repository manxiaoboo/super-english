## Chinese Intention

公司要求所有人做某事（全员要求）

## Natural Chunk

the company requires everyone to [do something]

## Avoid Saying

- the company require every one to / the company ask everyone to

## Sentence Patterns

- The company requires everyone to ...
- Our organization requires everyone to ... before ...
- The policy requires everyone to ...

## Real-Life Examples

- The company requires everyone to update their personal information annually.
- The company requires everyone to complete the security training before accessing the system.

## Usage Notes



## Production Prompts

1. 用 The company requires everyone to ... 造一句关于公司规定或政策的句子。
