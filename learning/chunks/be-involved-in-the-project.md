# be involved in the project

- 学习日期：2026-07-08

- 下次复习：2026-07-09
- 复习次数：0
- 最近结果：learned

## Chinese Intention

参与这个项目；和这个项目有关

## Natural Chunk

be involved in the project

## Avoid Saying

- join in the project

## Sentence Patterns

- How many people are involved in ...?
- I want to be involved in ...
- He was involved in ... from the beginning.

## Real-Life Examples

- How many people are involved in the project?
- I want to be involved in projects that improve real learning.

## Usage Notes

be involved in 强调已经参与其中，或和某件事存在实际关联。说 project, process, discussion, decision 时都很常见。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 How many people are involved in ...? 造一个和团队或项目有关的问题。
2. 用 I want to be involved in ... because ... 造一句和你的学习或工作有关的句子。
