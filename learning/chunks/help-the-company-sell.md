# help the company sell

## Chinese Intention

帮助公司卖出产品或推动销售

## Natural Chunk

help the company sell

## Avoid Saying

- help the company to sell

## Sentence Patterns

- Good ... can help the company sell ...
- ... helps the company sell ...

## Real-Life Examples

- Good advertising can help the company sell its products.
- Better packaging helps the company sell more units online.

## Usage Notes

这里常用 help + object + base verb，语气更紧凑自然。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 Good ... can help the company sell ... 造一句和产品或品牌有关的句子。
