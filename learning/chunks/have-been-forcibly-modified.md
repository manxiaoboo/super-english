## Chinese Intention

某东西被强制修改了（通用技术场景）

## Natural Chunk

have been forcibly modified

## Avoid Saying

- has been force modified

## Sentence Patterns

- [Something] have been forcibly modified without [authorization / notice].
- It appears that [something] have been forcibly modified by [cause].

## Real-Life Examples

- The configuration files have been forcibly modified by the deployment script.
- The menu bar styles have been forcibly modified by an external plugin.

## Usage Notes

forcibly is the adverb form of force. Never use force directly as an adverb before a past participle. forcibly modified = changed against the intended behavior or without authorization.

## Production Prompts

1. 用 have been forcibly modified 描述一次配置或样式被意外改动的情况。
