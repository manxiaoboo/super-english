# make the room look better

## Chinese Intention

让房间看起来更好或更舒服

## Natural Chunk

make the room look better

## Avoid Saying

- makes room looks good

## Sentence Patterns

- The placement of ... makes the room look better.
- ... makes the room look bigger.

## Real-Life Examples

- The placement of the picture on the wall makes the room look better.
- The placement of the chairs makes the room look bigger.

## Usage Notes

这里常用 make + object + base verb，后面接 look + adjective。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 The placement of ... makes the room look ... 造一句和家具或装修有关的句子。
