## Chinese Intention

有权限访问某个系统、服务器或资源

## Natural Chunk

have access to

## Avoid Saying

- can visit

## Sentence Patterns

- You can access ...
- You have access to ...

## Real-Life Examples

- You can find the Panzura server that you have access to.
- Only admins have access to this server.

## Usage Notes

说系统、服务器、平台、文件资源时，通常用 access / have access to，不用 visit。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 have access to 造一句和服务器、系统或文件有关的句子。
