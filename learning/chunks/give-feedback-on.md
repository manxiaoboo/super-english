## Chinese Intention

就某事给出反馈或评价

## Natural Chunk

give [someone] feedback on [something]

## Avoid Saying

- give feedback about / give feedback for

## Sentence Patterns

- Can you give me feedback on ...?
- I'll give you feedback on ... after ...
- She gave feedback on ... during the meeting.

## Real-Life Examples

- Can you give me feedback on my essay before I submit it?
- The manager gave feedback on the team's performance at the review.
- Please give feedback on the new design so we can improve it.
