## Chinese Intention

## Natural Chunk

resistance to change

## Avoid Saying

- resistance for change

## Sentence Patterns

- There is strong resistance to ...
- Many people show resistance to ... because ...

## Real-Life Examples

- There is strong resistance to the new workflow.

## Usage Notes

Use resistance to something, not resistance for something.

1. 用 There is strong resistance to ... 写一句关于工作或学习变化的句子。
