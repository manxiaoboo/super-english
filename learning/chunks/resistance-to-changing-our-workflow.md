## Avoid Saying

## Sentence Patterns

- There is strong resistance to ...
- We met with resistance to ...

## Real-Life Examples

- There is strong resistance to changing our workflow.
- We met with resistance to changing the old system.

## Usage Notes

resistance to 后面接动名词（-ing），不接动词原形。这是介词 to 的规则，同样适用于 committed to, opposed to, accustomed to。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 There is strong resistance to ... 造一句和团队、政策或系统改变有关的英文句子，注意 to 后面用 -ing。
