## Chinese Intention

表达某个系统或设备由多个模块组成

## Natural Chunk

consists of multiple modules

## Avoid Saying

- is consist of multipe modules

## Sentence Patterns

- The ... consists of multiple modules.
- This system consists of ...

## Real-Life Examples

- The equipment consists of multiple modules.
- This training program consists of three stages.

## Usage Notes

consist of 是固定结构，不要在前面再加 is。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 The ... consists of multiple modules. 造一句和系统、课程或团队有关的句子。
