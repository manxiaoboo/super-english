## Chinese Intention

会修 bug 的工程师

## Natural Chunk

the engineer who can fix bugs

## Avoid Saying

- the engineer who can fixes bugs
- the engineer who can fixing bugs

## Sentence Patterns

- The engineer who can ... is ...
- We need an engineer who can ...

## Real-Life Examples

- We need an engineer who can fix bugs quickly.

## Usage Notes

After can, use the base verb: can fix, not can fixes.

## Production Prompts

1. 用 We need an engineer who can ... 写一句关于团队招聘或协作的英文。
