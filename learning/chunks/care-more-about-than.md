# care more about ... than ...

## Chinese Intention

表达更看重 A 而不是 B

## Natural Chunk

care more about ... than ...

## Avoid Saying

- care the value more than name

## Sentence Patterns

- I care more about ... than ...
- Some customers care more about ... than ...

## Real-Life Examples

- I care more about value for money than brand names.
- Some customers care more about quality than fancy packaging.

## Usage Notes

后面常接名词短语，特别适合表达价值取向。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 I care more about ... than ... 造一句和购物或学习有关的句子。
