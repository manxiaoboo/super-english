## Chinese Intention

样式被强制覆盖了（前端/CSS 场景）

## Natural Chunk

styles were forcibly overridden

## Avoid Saying

- style has been force modified

## Sentence Patterns

- The [element] styles were forcibly overridden by [something].
- It looks like [element] styles have been forcibly overridden.

## Real-Life Examples

- The menu bar styles were forcibly overridden after the library update.
- The button styles were forcibly overridden by a third-party plugin.

## Usage Notes

In frontend/CSS contexts, overridden is more precise than modified — styles are typically overridden (covered by a higher-specificity rule), not edited in place. Use forcibly to indicate it happened without intent or authorization.

## Production Prompts

1. 用 were forcibly overridden 描述一个样式被意外覆盖的前端 bug。
