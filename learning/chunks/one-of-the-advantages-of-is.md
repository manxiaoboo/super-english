## Sentence Patterns

- One of the advantages of ... is that it can ...
- One of the main advantages of ... is ...

## Real-Life Examples

- One of the advantages of learning English is that it can improve my thinking ability.
- One of the main advantages of online learning is flexibility.

## Usage Notes

of 后面通常接名词或动名词，比如 learning, reading, working from home。
