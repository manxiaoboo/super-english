## Chinese Intention

AI 正在给我们的生活带来的变化

## Natural Chunk

the changes that AI is bringing to our lives

## Avoid Saying

- changes that AI bringning to our lives

## Sentence Patterns

- We need to understand ...
- People are talking about ...
- ... are affecting the way we ...

## Real-Life Examples

- We need to raise awareness of the changes that AI is bringing to our lives.
- People are talking more about the changes that AI is bringing to our lives.

## Usage Notes

适合表达 AI 对日常生活、工作或学习正在造成的具体变化。可以放在 understand, discuss, raise awareness of 这类结构后面。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 We need to understand ... 造一句和 AI 影响生活或学习有关的英文句子。
