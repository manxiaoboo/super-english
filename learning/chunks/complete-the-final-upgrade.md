## Chinese Intention

完成最后一次升级/迁移/发布

## Natural Chunk

complete the final [upgrade / migration / release]

## Avoid Saying

- complete the last time [upgrade]

## Sentence Patterns

- We have already completed the final [upgrade].
- Once we complete the final migration, we'll [do next step].

## Real-Life Examples

- We have already completed the final App-Shell upgrade in V2.
- The team completed the final migration last sprint.

## Usage Notes

Use 'final' to mean the last in a planned series. 'last time' is adverbial and cannot directly modify a noun.

## Production Prompts

你们完成了某个系列中最后一次升级，怎么说？
