## Chinese Intention

表达我在某个时刻认出了你

## Natural Chunk

recognised you when

## Avoid Saying

- recognised you when you say

## Sentence Patterns

- I recognised you when you ...
- I recognised ... immediately when ...

## Real-Life Examples

- I recognised you when you said hello to me this morning.
- I recognised his voice immediately when he called me.

## Usage Notes

讲过去某一刻认出来时，when 从句通常也跟过去时。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 I recognised you when you ... 造一句和日常生活有关的句子。
