## Chinese Intention

会说英语的女孩

## Natural Chunk

the girl who can speak English

## Avoid Saying

- the girl who can speaks English
- the girl who can speaking English

## Sentence Patterns

- The girl who can ... is ...
- I know a girl who can ...

## Real-Life Examples

- I know a girl who can speak English very well.

## Usage Notes

After can, use the base verb: can speak, not can speaks.

## Production Prompts

1. 用 I know a girl who can ... 写一句英文，注意 can 后面用动词原形。
