## Chinese Intention

遇到反对或阻力

## Natural Chunk

meet with resistance

## Avoid Saying

- meet resistance from people

## Sentence Patterns

- The plan met with resistance from ...
- The proposal may meet with resistance because ...

## Real-Life Examples

- The plan met with resistance from several developers.

## Usage Notes

meet with resistance is a natural formal/semi-formal chunk for plans, proposals, or changes that face opposition.

## Production Prompts

1. 用 The plan met with resistance from ... 写一句关于方案受阻的句子。
