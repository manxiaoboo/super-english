## Chinese Intention

建立并落实一个流程

## Natural Chunk

put a process in place

## Avoid Saying

- put a process in a place

## Sentence Patterns

- The team put ... in place to ...
- We should put ... in place before ...

## Real-Life Examples

- The team put a review process in place to reduce mistakes.

## Usage Notes

process is a natural object of put in place in work and project contexts.
