## Chinese Intention

落实保护措施或防护机制

## Natural Chunk

put safeguards in place

## Avoid Saying

- put safeguards in a place

## Sentence Patterns

- We need to put safeguards in place before ...
- Once safeguards are in place, ...

## Real-Life Examples

- We need to put safeguards in place before releasing the package.

## Usage Notes

safeguards means protective measures; put safeguards in place is common in technical, policy, and risk-control contexts.
