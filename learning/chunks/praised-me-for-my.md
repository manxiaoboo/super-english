## Chinese Intention

上司/老师表扬我某方面的表现

## Natural Chunk

praised me for my ___

## Avoid Saying

- give praise to me because performance is well

## Sentence Patterns

- My [manager/teacher] praised me for my ___ performance.
- [Someone] praised me for [doing sth].

## Real-Life Examples

- My team leader praised me for my strong performance in the last sprint.
- The teacher praised her for her clear explanation.
