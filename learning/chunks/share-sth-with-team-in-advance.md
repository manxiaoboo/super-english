## Chinese Intention

提前将信息共享给团队

## Natural Chunk

share [sth] with [team] in advance

## Avoid Saying

- provide [information] to [team] in advance

## Sentence Patterns

- We'd like to share [the new version information] with [the X team] in advance.
- Let me share [the updates] with the team in advance so they can prepare.

## Real-Life Examples

- We'd like to share the new version information with the Trend, Mantis, and UDA teams in advance.
- I'll share the release notes with the QA team in advance before the deployment.

## Usage Notes

share ... with is more natural than provide ... to when proactively informing teams; provide is better for formal deliverables.

## Production Prompts

你想提前告诉某个团队一些信息，怎么说？
