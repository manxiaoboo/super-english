# become aware of the power of AI

## Chinese Intention

逐渐意识到 AI 的力量或影响

## Natural Chunk

become aware of the power of AI

## Avoid Saying

- awareness of the powers of the AI

## Sentence Patterns

- I am gradually becoming aware of ...
- People are becoming more aware of ...

## Real-Life Examples

- I am gradually becoming aware of the power of AI.
- More people are becoming aware of the power of AI in education.

## Usage Notes

当你想表达“慢慢意识到”时，用 become aware of 很自然。awareness 更常作名词，放在 build awareness of, raise awareness of 这类结构里。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 I am gradually becoming aware of ... 造一句和学习、工作或 AI 有关的英文句子。
