## Chinese Intention

系统会自动开始起作用

## Natural Chunk

the system kicks in automatically

## Avoid Saying

- I kick in the system automatically

## Sentence Patterns

- The system kicks in automatically when ...
- Once ..., the system kicks in automatically.

## Real-Life Examples

- The system kicks in automatically when a risky permission change is detected.

## Usage Notes

The subject is the system, not the person. Use this for automatic mechanisms that start working under certain conditions.

## Production Prompts
