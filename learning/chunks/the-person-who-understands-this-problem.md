## Chinese Intention

理解这个问题的人

## Natural Chunk

the person who understands this problem

## Avoid Saying

- the person who understands this question

## Sentence Patterns

- The person who ... can ...
- I need someone who ...

## Real-Life Examples

- The person who understands this problem can fix it quickly.

## Usage Notes

Use problem for a bug, difficulty, or technical issue; question is usually something you ask or answer.

## Production Prompts

1. 用 The person who ... can ... 写一句关于解决技术问题的英文。
