## Chinese Intention

给你做某事的精力

## Natural Chunk

give you the energy to

## Avoid Saying

- provide the energy to you to support your working

## Sentence Patterns

- ... can give you the energy to ...
- Breakfast gives me the energy to ...

## Real-Life Examples

- Having breakfast can give you the energy to work through the whole day.
- A short break can give you the energy to focus again.

## Usage Notes

表达“给你精力去做某事”时，give you the energy to ... 比 provide the energy to you to ... 自然得多。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 ... can give you the energy to ... 造一句和生活或学习有关的句子。
