# conduct a survey to understand students better

## Chinese Intention

进行一项调查来更好地了解学生

## Natural Chunk

conduct a survey to understand students better

## Avoid Saying

- conducting survey to realize his students

## Sentence Patterns

- My teacher is conducting a survey to ...
- We are conducting a survey to understand ...

## Real-Life Examples

- My teacher is conducting a survey to understand his students better.
- The company is conducting a survey to understand its users better.

## Usage Notes

realize = 意识到/突然明白，不等于'了解'。想表达'了解某人/某事'时，优先用 understand 或 get to know。conduct a survey 是固定搭配，survey 前要有 a。

## Production Prompts

1. 用 We are conducting a survey to understand ... 造一句和学生、用户或团队有关的英文句子。
