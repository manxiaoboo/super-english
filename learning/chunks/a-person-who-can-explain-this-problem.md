## Chinese Intention

能解释这个问题的人

## Natural Chunk

a person who can explain this problem

## Avoid Saying

- a person who can explains the problem
- a person who can explaining this problem

## Sentence Patterns

- We need a person who can ...
- I need someone who can ...

## Real-Life Examples

- We need a person who can explain this problem clearly.

## Usage Notes

After can, use the base verb: can explain, not can explains.

## Production Prompts
