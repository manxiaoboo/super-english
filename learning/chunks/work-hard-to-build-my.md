## Chinese Intention

努力做某事（持续付出努力去提升或建立某项能力）

## Natural Chunk

work hard to build my ___

## Avoid Saying

- build my ___ very hard

## Sentence Patterns

- I'm working hard to build my ___.
- She's been working hard to build her ___ in ___.

## Real-Life Examples

- I'm working hard to build my vocabulary.
- He's working hard to build his reputation as a reliable engineer.
