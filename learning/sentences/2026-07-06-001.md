## Sentence Breakdown

### Main Clause

A brand is the expression

### Modifiers / Extra Information

- of essential characteristics and values: explains what is being expressed
- of a product or service: explains whose characteristics and values they are

### Chunk Meaning

- A brand = 一个品牌
- is the expression of = 是……的体现
- essential characteristics and values = 核心特征和价值
- of a product or service = 某个产品或服务的

整体关系：品牌 = 产品或服务核心特征和价值的外在体现。
